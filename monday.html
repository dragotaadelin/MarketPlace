Home > Rolex > Day-Date 36

Details

Launched in 1956, the Day-Date made its debut as the first waterproof and self-winding chronometer wristwatch to offer a modern calendar with an instantaneous day display, spelt out in a window on the dial, in addition to the date.

The exceptional precision, reliability, legibility and presence of this prestigious model has made it the ultimate status watch.
